Lions

Here are some fan facts about lions.

  • Lions weigh up to 250 kgs.
  • A lion's roar can be heard upto 8km away.
  • Female lions hunt, while male lions guard their territorry and their cubs.

What to do if a lion sneaks up on you.

  1. Freeze.
  2. Don't make eye contact.
  3. Wave your arms above your head and scream as loud as you can!
